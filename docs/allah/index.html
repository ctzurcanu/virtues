The 99 Names of Allah (Al Asma Ul Husna) as Virtues

Allah (سُبْحَٰنَهُۥ وَتَعَٰلَىٰ) names and attributes can be understood as the perfect implementation of Virtues.

Virtues -> Names, Attributes​

1. God, The Destroyer​

    1. Al-Mumeet (ٱلْمُمِيتُ) The Bringer of Death, the Destroyer
    1. Al-Qahhar (ٱلْقَهَّارُ) The Subduer, The Ever-Dominating
    1. Al-Mutakabbir (ٱلْمُتَكَبِّرُ) The Supreme, The Majestic
    1. Al-Jabbar (ٱلْجَبَّارُ) The Compeller, The Restorer

    1. Ad-Dharr (ٱلْضَّارُ) The Distresser

    1. Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ) The All-Wise
    1. Al-Ba’Ith (ٱلْبَاعِثُ) The Resurrector, The Raiser of the Dead

8 Mercy, Compassion​

    1. Ar-Rahman (The Beneficent): He who wills goodness and mercy for all His creatures
    1. Ar-Raheem (The Merciful): He who acts with extreme kindness
    1. Al-Ghaffar (The Great Forgiver): The Forgiver, The One who forgives the sins of His slaves time and time again.
    1. Al-Fattah (The Opener): The Solver, The Reliever, The Judge, The One who opens for His slaves the closed worldly and religious matters.
    1. Al-Ghafoor (The All-Forgiving): The All-Forgiving, The Forgiving, The One who forgives a lot.
    1. Al-Barr (The Fountain-Head of Truth): The Source of All Goodness, The Righteous, The One who is kind to His creatures
    1. At-Tawwab (The Ever-Acceptor of Repentance): The Acceptor of Repentance, The Oft-Forgiving, The Acceptor of our Return
    1. Al-Afuw (The Supreme Pardoner): The Pardoner, The Forgiver and The Eliminator of Sins.

11 Knowledge, Wisdom​

    1. Al-Alim (The All-Knowing One): The Knowledgeable; The One nothing is absent from His knowledge
    1. As-Sami (The All-Hearer): The Hearer, The One who Hears all things that are heard by His Eternal Hearing without an ear, instrument or organ.
    1. Al-Baseer (The All-Seeing): The All-Noticing, The One who Sees all things that are seen by His Eternal Seeing without a pupil or any other instrument.
    1. Al-Khabir (The All-Aware One): The One who knows the truth of things.
    1. Al-Haleem (The Clement One): The Forebearing, The One who delays the punishment for those who deserve it and then He might forgive them.
    1. Al-Haseeb (The Reckoning One): The Reckoner, The One who gives the satisfaction.
    1. Al-Wasi (The All-Pervading One): The Vast, The All-Embracing, The Knowledgeable.
    1. Al-Hakeem (The Wise One): The Wise, The Judge of Judges, The One who is correct in His doings.
    1. Ash-Shaheed (The All Observing Witness): The Witness, The One who nothing is absent from Him.
    1. Al-Muhsi (The All-Enumerating One): The Counter, The Reckoner, The One who the count of things are known to him.
    1. Al-Wajid (The Pointing One): The Perceiver, The Finder, The Rich who is never poor. Al-Wajid is Richness.

13 Power, Might​

    1. Al-Aziz (The Mighty One): The Strong, The Defeater who is not defeated
    1. Al-Jabbar (The All Compelling): The One that nothing happens in His Dominion except that which He willed
    1. Al-Qahhar (The All-Prevailing One): The Dominant, The One who has the perfect Power and is not unable over anything.
    1. Al-Azeem (The Magnificent One): The Great One, The Mighty, The One deserving the attributes of Exaltment, Glory, Extolement, and Purity from all imperfection.
    1. Al-Kabeer (The Great One): The Most Great, The Great, The One who is greater than everything in status.
    1. Al-Qawiyy (The Strong One): The Most Strong, The Strong, The One with the complete Power.
    1. Al-Matin (The Firm One): The One with extreme Power which is un-interrupted and He does not get tired.
    1. Al-Waliy (The Protecting Associate): The Protecting Friend, The Supporter.
    1. Al-Mumeet (The Inflictor of Death): The Creator of Death, The Destroyer, The One who renders the living dead.
    1. Al-Qadir (The All-Powerful): The Able, The Capable, The One attributed with Power.
    1. Al-Muqtadir (The All Authoritative One): The Powerful, The Dominant, The One with the perfect Power that nothing is withheld from Him.
    1. Al-Muqaddim (The Expediting One): The Expediter, The Promoter, The One who puts things in their right places.
    1. Al-Wali (The Holder of Supreme Authority): The Governor, The One who owns things and manages them.

9 Perfection​

    1. Al-Malik (The King): The Sovereign Lord, The One with the complete Dominion, the One Whose Dominion is clear from imperfection
    1. Al-Quddus (The Most Sacred / The Most Holy): The one who is pure, so far removed of any imperfection. He has no shortcomings, evil is not attributed to Him.
    1. As-Salam (The Giver of Peace): The One who is free from every imperfection. It is because He is free of any defect that one who is with Him experiences tranquility.
    1. Al-Wajid (The Pointing One): The Perceiver, The Finder, The Rich who is never poor. Al-Wajid is Richness.
    1. As-Samad (The Supreme Provider): The Eternal, The Independent, The Master who is relied upon in matters and reverted to in ones needs. He is the only one a person should turn to when feeling helpless.
    1. Malikul-Mulk (The Eternal Possessor of Sovereignty): The One who controls the Dominion and gives dominion to whoever He willed.
    1. Al-Ghaniy (The Self-Sufficient One): The One who does not need the creation.
    1. Al-Mughni (The Enricher): The Emancipator, The Fulfiller of Needs, The Bestower of Wealth.
    1. An-Noor (The Prime Light): The Light, The One who guides.

13 Nobility, Majesty, Glory​

    1. Al-Quddus (The Most Sacred / The Most Holy): The one who is pure, so far removed of any imperfection. He has no shortcomings, evil is not attributed to Him.
    1. Al-Mutakabbir (The Dominant One): The One who is clear from the attributes of the creatures and from resembling them.
    1. Al-Azeem (The Magnificent One): The Great One, The Mighty, The One deserving the attributes of Exaltment, Glory, Extolement, and Purity from all imperfection.
    1. Al-Aliyy (The Sublime One): The Most High, The One who is clear from the attributes of the creatures.
    1. Al-Kabeer (The Great One): The Most Great, The Great, The One who is greater than everything in status.
    1. Al-Jaleel (The Majestic One): The Sublime One, The Beneficent, The One who is attributed with greatness of Power and Glory of status.
    1. Al-Majeed (The Glorious One): The Most Glorious One, The One who is with perfect Power, High Status, Compassion, Generosity and Kindness.
    1. Al-Hameed (The Sole-Laudable One): The Praiseworthy, The praised One who deserves to be praised.
    1. Al-Maajid (The All-Noble One): The Glorious, He who is Most Glorious.
    1. Az-Zaahir (The Perceptible): The Manifest, The Conspicuous, The Evident
    1. Al-Baatin (The Imperceptible): The Hidden, The Secret One, The Inner One, The Knower of Hidden Things
    1. Al-Mutaali (The Extremely Exalted One): The Most Exalted, The High Exalted, The One who is clear from the attributes of the creation.
    1. Dhul-Jalaali-Wal-Ikram (The Possessor of Majesty and Honour): The Lord of Majesty and Bounty.

17 Sublimity, Benevolence​

    1. Al-Mumin (The Infuser of Faith): The One who witnessed for Himself that no one is God but Him. And He witnessed for His believers that they are truthful in their belief that no one is God but Him
    1. Al-Wahhab (The Supreme Bestower): The One who is Generous in giving plenty without any return. He is everything that benefits whether Halal or Haram.
    1. Ar-Razzaq (The Total Provider): The Sustainer, The Provider.
    1. Al-Fattah (The Opener): The Solver, The Reliever, The Judge, The One who opens for His slaves the closed worldly and religious matters.
    1. Al-Lateef (The Knower of Subtleties): The Subtle One, The Gracious, The One who is kind to His slaves and endows upon them.
    1. Al-Haleem (The Clement One): The Forebearing, The One who delays the punishment for those who deserve it and then He might forgive them.
    1. Ash-Shakur (The Acknowledging One): The Grateful, The Appreciative, The One who gives a lot of reward for a little obedience.
    1. Al-Muqeet (The Sustaining One): The Maintainer, The Guardian, The Feeder, The One who has the Power.
    1. Al-Karim (The Bountiful One): The Generous One, The Gracious, The One who is attributed with greatness of Power and Glory of status.
    1. Al-Mujeeb (The Responding One): The Responsive, The Hearkener, The One who answers the one in need if he asks Him and rescues the yearned if he calls upon Him.
    1. Al-Wadud (The Loving One): The Most Loving, The Most Affectionate, The Beloved
    1. Ar-Rauf (The Benign One): The Most Kind, The Tenderly Merciful, The Clement and Compassionate
    1. Al-Muqsit (The Just One): The Equitable, The One who is Just in His judgment.
    1. Al-Jami (The Assembler of Scattered Creations): The Gatherer, The One who gathers the creatures on a day that there is no doubt about, that is the Day of Judgment.
    1. Al-Hadi (The Provider of Guidance): The Guide, The Leader, The Guide of the Right Path
    1. Ar-Rasheed (The Guide to Path of Rectitude): The Guide to the Right Path, The One who guides.
    1. As-Sabur (The Extensively Enduring One): The Patient, The One who does not quickly punish the sinners.

7 Guardianship, Support​

    1. Al-Mumin (The Infuser of Faith): The One who witnessed for Himself that no one is God but Him. And He witnessed for His believers that they are truthful in their belief that no one is God but Him
    1. Al-Muhaymin (The Guardian): The One who witnesses the saying and deeds of His creatures.
    1. Al-Hafiz (The Guarding One): The Preserver, The Protector, The One who protects whatever and whoever He willed to protect.
    1. Ar-Raqib (The Watchful One): The Watcher, The One that nothing is absent from Him. Hence, it’s meaning is related to attribute of Knowledge.
    1. Al-Wakeel (The Universal Trustee): The Trustee, The One who gives the satisfaction and is relied upon.
    1. Al-Waliy (The Protecting Associate): The Protecting Friend, The Supporter.
    1. As-Samad (The Supreme Provider): The Eternal, The Independent, The Master who is relied upon in matters and reverted to in ones needs. He is the only one a person should turn to when feeling helpless.

6 Creation​

    1. Al-Khaliq (The Creator): The One who brings everything from non-existence to existence
    1. Al-Bari (The Evolver / The Maker): The Maker, The Creator who has the Power to turn the entities.
    1. Al-Musawwir (The Flawless Shaper): The One who forms His creatures in different pictures.
    1. Al-Mubdi (The Originator): The One who started the human being. That is, He created him.
    1. Al-Muid (The Restorer): The Reproducer, The One who brings back the creatures after death.
    1. Al-Badi (The Unique One): The Wonderful Originator, The Unprecedented and Incomparable Inventor

3 Oneness, Uniqueness​

    1. Al-Wahid (The Only One): The Unique, The One, The One without a partner.
    1. Al-Ahad (The Sole One): The One.
    1. Al-Warith (The Eternal Inheritor): The Heir, The One whose Existence remains.

6 Existence​

    1. Al-Haqq (The Embodiment of Truth): The Truth, The True, The One who truly exists.
    1. Al-Hayy (The Eternally Living One): The Alive, The One attributed with a life that is unlike our life and is not that of a combination of soul, flesh or blood.
    1. Al-Qayyum (The Self-Subsisting One): The Self-Subsisting, The Self-Existing One upon Whom all others depend
    1. Al-Awwal (The Very First): The First, The One whose Existence is without a beginning.
    1. Al-Akhir (The Infinite Last One): The Last, The One whose Existence is without an end.
    1. Al-Baaqi (The Ever Surviving One): The Everlasting, The Ever-Enduring, The Ever-Present

6 Dominion​

    1. Al-Malik (The King): The Sovereign Lord, The One with the complete Dominion, the One Whose Dominion is clear from imperfection
    1. Al-Qahhar (The All-Prevailing One): The Dominant, The One who has the perfect Power and is not unable over anything.
    1. Al-Hakam (The Impartial Judge): The Judge, He is the Ruler andThe Judge, He is the Ruler and His judgment is His Word. His judgment is His Word.
    1. Al-Adl (The Embodiment of Justice): The Just, The One who is entitled to do what He does.
    1. Malikul-Mulk (The Eternal Possessor of Sovereignty): The One who controls the Dominion and gives dominion to whoever He willed.
    1. Dhul-Jalaali-Wal-Ikram (The Possessor of Majesty and Honour): The Lord of Majesty and Bounty.

14 Allah's Will​

    1. Al-Qabid (The Restricting One): The Constrictor, The Withholder, The One who constricts the sustenance by His wisdom and expands and widens it with His Generosity and Mercy.
    1. Al-Basit (The Extender): The Englarger, The One who constricts the sustenance by His wisdom and expands and widens it with His Generosity and Mercy.
    1. Al-Khafid (The Reducer): The Abaser, The One who lowers whoever He willed by His Destruction and raises whoever He willed by His Endowment.
    1. Ar-Rafi (The Elevating One): The Exalter, The Elevator, The One who lowers whoever He willed by His Destruction and raises whoever He willed by His Endowment.
    1. Al-Mu'izz (The Honourer-Bestower): He gives esteem to whoever He willed, hence there is no one to degrade Him; And He degrades whoever He willed, hence there is no one to give Him esteem.
    1. Al-Muzil (The Abaser): The Dishonourer, The Humiliator, He gives esteem to whoever He willed, hence there is no one to degrade Him; And He degrades whoever He willed, hence there is no one to give Him esteem.
    1. Al-Ba'ith (The Infuser of New Life ): The Awakener, The Resurrector, The Arouser
    1. Al-Muhyi (The Maintainer of life): The Restorer, The Giver of Life.
    1. Al-Mumeet (The Inflictor of Death): The Creator of Death, The Destroyer, The One who renders the living dead.
    1. Al-Muqaddim (The Expediting One): The Expediter, The Promoter, The One who puts things in their right places.
    1. Al-Muakhkhir (The Delayer): The Postponer, He Who Puts Far Away. He makes ahead what He wills and delays what He wills.
    1. Al-Mani (The Preventer): The Withholder.
    1. Ad-Darr (The Distressor): The Correcter, The Balancer, The Distresser, The Afflicter, The Punisher.
    1. An-Naafi (The Bestower of Benefits): The Creator of Good, The Benefiter, The Propitious, The Auspicious
